It was a dark and stormy night..

The clumsy elves that used to wake her from sleep had learned to be more careful.

They faded into a distant memory only to be replaced by an army of leprechauns that came with pots of gold.

The girl had had enough of these impish invaders disturbing her slumber. She went to the hearth and called out to the soot sprites she had befreinded and negotiated with them a pact to guard her home in exchange for a gift.
